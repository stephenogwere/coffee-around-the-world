About Us

  • Coffee around the World offers coffee tasting and storytelling in Nairobi, Kenya. We promote the exploration of global coffee recipes and the stories and cultural wealth that come with such unique coffee types.

  • We facilitate socializing and networking among coffee lovers in Nairobi. Partnering with pronounced coffee houses, we deliver exquisite coffee tasting sessions coupled with storytelling, book reading/launches and cultural dance performances among other activities.

  • Join us for one of our events and become a member to benefit from discounted event access fees and reap discounts from coffee purchases from our partner cafes in Nairobi.
